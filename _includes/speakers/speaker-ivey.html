Advancing Responsible AI & AI for Good

Ivey is the Sr Manager of AI Research Partnerships & AI for Good with the TELUS AI Accelerator Team. She works on a diverse portfolio of data science and AI projects including those in Responsible AI, People Analytics and Sustainability. Her AI for Good team has the distinction of winning two consecutive Industry Sustainability awards from the Global Generative AI Association (2024, 2025), demonstrating TELUS' commitment to use its technology for good.

She has been accorded a Special Jury Recognition by the Women in AI Association (Canada-2022) for her industry innovations in AI. She has also been recognized for her inclusive leadership with the TELUS CHLOE Champion of Diversity, Equality and Inclusion award, and for her ability to tell the story of the TELUS AI journey with the 2024 TELUS Social Media Influencer Award. She is active in the Technology & Engineering Community (TEC) at TELUS as an executive member and served as VP and President of TEC from 2023-2024.

About Our Speaker

Ivey Chiu brings extensive expertise in AI research, responsible AI practices, and AI for Good initiatives. Her work at TELUS focuses on developing and deploying AI solutions that create positive social impact while maintaining the highest standards of ethics and responsibility.

As a panelist at the 35th annual IEEE CASCON Conference (2025), Ivey participated in discussions on "The Future of Software Engineering in GenAI Era". The panel explored how Generative AI is transforming software development, discussing its impact on methodologies, innovation acceleration, and the evolving skill sets required for software engineers.

Her leadership in the Technology & Engineering Community at TELUS and her recognition for diversity and inclusion work demonstrate her dedication to creating inclusive environments where technology serves all members of society. Through her role at the TELUS AI Accelerator, Ivey helps bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI research and practical, ethical applications that benefit both businesses and communities.
