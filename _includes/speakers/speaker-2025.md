## Guest Speaker Series 2025

We are excited to welcome a diverse lineup of industry leaders and successful entrepreneurs to share their insights with DCSIL students this term.

### Featured Speakers

Our guest speaker program brings together founders, executives, and technical leaders who have built and scaled technology companies. These sessions provide students with real-world perspectives on entrepreneurship, product development, and business strategy.

Each speaker session is designed to be interactive, allowing students to engage directly with industry veterans and ask questions about their experiences, challenges, and lessons learned.

### Upcoming Sessions

Details about upcoming guest speaker sessions will be announced throughout the term. Students enrolled in DCSIL courses will receive notifications about speaker schedules and topics.

### Past Highlights

Our guest speaker program has featured leaders from various sectors including artificial intelligence, fintech, healthcare technology, and enterprise software. These sessions have provided students with valuable insights into different aspects of building and scaling technology companies.
