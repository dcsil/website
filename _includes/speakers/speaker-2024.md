## Guest Speaker Series 2024

Throughout the 2024 academic year, DCSIL hosted a series of guest speakers who shared their expertise and experiences with our students.

### Speaker Topics

Our 2024 guest speaker series covered a wide range of topics including:

- **Product Strategy and Market Fit**: Speakers discussed how to identify market opportunities and develop products that resonate with customers.

- **Technical Architecture at Scale**: Industry leaders shared insights on building systems that can handle growth and maintain reliability.

- **Fundraising and Investor Relations**: Entrepreneurs who successfully raised capital provided guidance on navigating the fundraising process.

- **Team Building and Culture**: Founders discussed how to build and maintain high-performing teams in fast-growing companies.

### Key Takeaways

Students gained valuable insights into the practical aspects of entrepreneurship, from initial product development through scaling and growth. The interactive format of these sessions allowed for deep dives into specific challenges and opportunities that students were facing in their own projects.
