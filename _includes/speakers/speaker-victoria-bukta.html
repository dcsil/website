About Our Speaker

As an alumnus of the University of Toronto, Victoria specialized in Computer Science, with a keen interest in AI and Operating Systems. Her background in operating systems provides a unique perspective within the big data landscape; she believes every challenge in this space can be viewed as a systems problem, solvable by breaking it down into core algorithms and methodologies learned in theoretical courses.

Having been a student in CSC 491 and a TA years ago, Victoria is excited to return and incorporate her product background into the course. Her journey from technical roles to product management gives her a comprehensive understanding of both the engineering and business aspects of building successful products.

Victoria's unique combination of technical depth and product management experience makes her an ideal instructor for students looking to understand how to bridge the gap between building technical products and successfully bringing them to market.
