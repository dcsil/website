Janneke Ritchie
Guest Speaker

An emerging voice on human judgement in the age of AI.

Janneke’s work sits at the intersection of AI and robotics, digital literacy, and human-centered technology—helping people understand emerging tools with clarity and confidence, and exploring how we can maintain (and strengthen) human connection as the world becomes increasingly digital. Her work explores why conversational AI often feels smarter and more trustworthy than it is — and how people judge the credibility of AI systems based on signals like language fluency, confident tone, and social cues.

About Our Speaker

Janneke Ritchie is the founder and CEO of Orange Gate Labs, a company that specializes in developing customized apps and using digital technologies to foster human connection. She is an innovator, designer, and educator who is passionate about empowering people to use modern technology with confidence and agility, particularly focusing on helping the Silver Set (people 50+) thrive in the digital age.

Currently, Janneke is pursuing a full-time Master's degree at the University of Toronto, where she is focusing on how social values manifest in AI and exploring the human understanding of artificial intelligence. Her academic pursuits align with her professional interests, as she often discusses the intersection of humanity and technology.
